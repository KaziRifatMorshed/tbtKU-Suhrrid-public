tbtKU Suhrrid, a project aimed at streamlining the student experience at Khulna University, was developed as part of the Database Systems Project/Fieldwork (CSE 2206) course within the Computer Science and Engineering Discipline. Developed by Kazi Rifat Morshed (230220), Md Rimon Islam (230236), and Md Mahfuzur Rahman (230228) under the guidance of Assistant Professor Aminul Islam, Suhrrid provides a platform for students to easily find housing and buy or sell secondhand goods. The development period ran from February to July 2025.
